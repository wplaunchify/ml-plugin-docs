## FluentCommunity Onboarding Guide

**Source:** [https://fluentcommunity.co/docs/fluentcommunity-onboarding-guide/](https://fluentcommunity.co/docs/fluentcommunity-onboarding-guide/)

Getting started with FluentCommunity is simple and intuitive. Once installed, you’ll be guided through the onboarding process to create and customize your community. Follow this step-by-step guide to set up your FluentCommunity.

## Set Up Your Community

- After installing FluentCommunity, you’ll be prompted to name your community. Enter a name that reflects your community’s purpose.
- Configure your **Community Portal URL** to define how users will access your FluentCommunity. You can customize the URL to suit your preferences.
- Add a logo for your community by clicking the **Update Logo** button. Upload your logo from your device to personalize your portal.
- Click **Continue to Next** to move to the next step.

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/Onboarding-1-scaled.webp)

#### Choose a Template

- Select a template for your community. Four options are available:
- **Basic**
- **Course**
- **Product**
- **Blank**
- Pick the template that best suits your needs, then click **Continue to Next** to proceed.

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/Onboarding-2-scaled.webp)

#### Configure Access & Features

In the next step, you’ll see options to fine-tune access and enable key features:

- **Who Can Access Your Portal**: Choose who can view your community portal by selecting your audience from the dropdown menu.
- **Integrated Marketing Automation**: If check this checkbox then the FluentCRM plugin will be automatically installed on your site to send targeted onboarding emails, marketing emails and run automation to your community members.
- **Reliable Email Delivery with FluentSMTP**: If you check this checkbox then FluentSMTP will be installed to ensure smooth email notifications and other email services for your community.
- **Feature Updates via Email**: Stay informed about the latest features and updates by providing your name and email address.
- **Subscribe to Newsletter**: Opt into the newsletter to receive regular updates.
- **Improve the Product**: Help enhance FluentCommunity by sharing non-sensitive data, such as member and space counts.

Once you’ve completed these settings, click **Save & Finish** to finalize your onboarding.

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/Onboarding-3-scaled.webp)

#### Access Your Community Portal

- After completing the onboarding process, you’ll see the **FluentCommunity Portal** entry page. Click **Go to the Portal** to access your community.
- Join the **Official FluentCommunity** by clicking the **Join Official Community** button to connect with other users and stay informed.

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/Onboarding-4-scaled.webp)

That’s it! Your FluentCommunity onboarding is complete. If you have any questions or need assistance with the onboarding process, feel free to contact our [support team](https://wpmanageninja.com/support-tickets/?utm_source=wpmn&utm_medium=home&utm_campaign=site#/).

## Global Activity Feed

**Source:** [https://fluentcommunity.co/docs/global-activity-feed/](https://fluentcommunity.co/docs/global-activity-feed/)

The Global Activity Feed in FluentCommunity lets members stay updated and interact with all recent activities across public and joined spaces. This central hub displays the latest posts, comments, and reactions, helping members stay connected and engaged with the community.

## Key Features of the Activity Feed

**Posts & Updates:** Members can share text, images, videos, or polls directly to the spaces they’re part of or post them on their profile for everyone to view. All shared content appears in the Activity Feed, allowing for quick and easy access to updates.

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/Global-Feed-Post-.webp)

**Comments & Reactions:** Engage with posts using comments and quick reactions like likes or emojis, encouraging interaction within the community.

#### Using the Global Activity Feed

**Viewing the Feed:** When members log in, they see a chronological list of recent updates from spaces they belong to.

**Trending Posts:** The right sidebar displays top-liked and most-commented posts, highlighting trending content within the community.

**Recent Activities:** View the latest posts across all joined spaces in one place for easy browsing.

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/Global-Feed-Recent-and-Trending-scaled.webp)

#### Benefits

**Real-time Updates:** Receive live updates from across the community to stay informed.

**Increased Interaction:** The feed promotes engagement through posts, comments, and reactions, keeping the community active and connected.

The Global Activity Feed creates an interactive space for members to share, connect, and engage, enhancing community involvement and keeping everyone in the loop. If you need further assistance, please [contact us](https://wpmanageninja.com/support-tickets/).

## Introduction to FluentCommunity

**Source:** [https://fluentcommunity.co/docs/introduction-to-fluent-community/](https://fluentcommunity.co/docs/introduction-to-fluent-community/)

**FluentCommunity** is a powerful WordPress plugin that transforms your website into a fully-featured community platform. It gives your members the ability to connect, share, and engage directly on your site. Whether it’s posting updates, sharing photos, or creating polls FluentCommunity brings everyone together in an interactive space designed to grow your community.

With features like public and private communities, course management, and direct messaging, you can fully control how your community operates.

## FluentCommunity Spaces

**FluentCommunity Spaces** lets you create and manage multiple communities for your site’s users. You can easily control who has access to each community and decide who gets added. Inside each community, members will enjoy the features and limitations you set.

You can create **Public**, **Private**, and **Secret** communities, tailoring the access levels and visibility to fit your needs. This feature allows you to oversee multiple communities and manage interactions exactly how you want.

## FluentCommunity Courses

**FluentCommunity Course** is a great feature that enables you to create, manage, and share courses within your community. You can design and organize your courses with a variety of customization options, ensuring a well-structured and engaging learning experience.

Enrollment is made easy for your community members, providing a smooth process for joining courses without any hassle. You can assign **Course** **Admins** and **Moderators** to help manage and oversee your courses, ensuring they run smoothly and effectively.

The plugin offers flexible privacy settings for your courses. You can choose from **Public** (open to everyone), **Private** (invite-only or restricted access), or **Secret** (completely hidden unless invited). Additionally, you can group courses and define different **course types** to enhance the organization and functionality of your course system.

## FluentCommunity Leaderboard

**FluentCommunity** also lets you create a **Leaderboard** to showcase member engagement and award titles. You can set up titles and assign point values, which help in promoting members to higher community ranks.

Members earn points through their interactions and liking content. This system encourages active participation and helps highlight top contributors in your community.

FluentCommunity helps you build and manage engaging communities directly on your WordPress site. With Spaces for interaction, Courses for learning, and Leaderboards for recognizing top members, it turns your site into a lively, interactive hub.

## Install FluentCommunity

**Source:** [https://fluentcommunity.co/docs/install-fluent-community/](https://fluentcommunity.co/docs/install-fluent-community/)

This article will guide you through **installing**, **activating**, and **updating**the **FluentCommunity** (**Free** and **Pro** versions) plugin on your **WordPress Site**. Please follow the instructions for a successful installation.

## Installing and Activating FluentCommunity

To learn how to install and activate the **Free Version of  FluentCommunity Plugin** directly from the WordPress Site, follow the steps with the screenshots below –

First, go to the **Add New Plugin** page from the **WordPress** **Dashboard**, search for **FluentCommunity**through the **Search Plugins** field, and the plugin will appear.

Now, click the **Install** button and the **FluentCommunity**plugin will be successfully **installed** on your WordPress site.

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/Instalaltion-1-scaled.webp)

Finally, to activate the FluentCommunity plugin, click the **Activate** button.

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/Instalaltion-2-scaled.webp)

#### Installing & Activating FluentCommunity Pro

To learn how to install and activate the **Pro Version of  FluentCommunity Plugin**, follow the steps with the screenshots below –

> Before starting to install and activate the FluentCommunity Pro version, you must have the .zip file of the FluentCommunity Pro Plugin.

#### Get the Zip File of FluentCommunity Pro

To get the zip file, first purchase the pro plugin [from here](https://fluentcommunity.co/).

Or, simply go to the **Dashboard** of **FluentCommunity’s** free version and click on the **Upgrade**button to purchase the Pro version.

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/Instalaltion-3-scaled.webp)

Once you have purchased the plugin, you will get access to the [WP Manage Ninja Dashboard](https://wpmanageninja.com/). Now, log into the [account](https://wpmanageninja.com/account/dashboard/) with your credentials.

Now, from the left sidebar, click **Download** to get the **FluentCommunity Pro** 
```
.zip
```

 file.

Next, click **Licenses** in the left sidebar to view your FluentCommunity license key. Copy the license key from this page. You can also see all sites activated with this license key by clicking the **View List** button.

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/FluentCommunity-License-scaled.webp)

#### Install the FluentCommunity Pro

Once you get the zip file, go to the **Plugins** from your **WordPress Dashboard**, and click on **Add New Plugin**.

Now, click the **Upload** **Plugin** button. Then, press the **Choose file** to upload the **FluentCommunity** **Pro** **.zip** file that you’ve downloaded before, and press the **Install Now**button.

The **FluentCommunity** **Pro** plugin will be successfully installed on your WordPress site!

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/Instalaltion-5-scaled.webp)

Now, to activate the FluentCommunity Pro plugin, click the **Activate** **Plugin**button.

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/Instalaltion-6.webp)

### Activate the License Key

To activate all the Pro features of FluentCommunity with the license key, follow the steps

First, go to **WordPress Dashboard** and click on the **FluentCommunity** from the right sidebar of **WordPress**, now you will see the FluentCommunity onboarding page. Click on the License Management button here.

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/FC-Licence--scaled.webp)

On the License Management page, paste the license key from your WPManageNinja account and click **Verify** **License**. Once activated, you’ll receive all the latest FluentCommunity updates.

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/FC-Licence-1-1.webp)

## How to Update FluentCommunity

For updating with the latest version of FluentCommunity, we have implemented an automatic update system compatible with the previous version. Also, you can manually update it anytime if needed.

To Update the FluentCommunity plugin manually, follow the steps in the screenshot below:

Go to the **Installed Plugins**page of your **WordPress Dashboard** and search for **FluentCommunity** from the Search Bar in the top right corner.

Now, check if any update is available. If yes, just click on the **Update** button.

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/Instalaltion-7-scaled.webp)
