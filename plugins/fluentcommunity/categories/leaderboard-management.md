## Leaderboard Management

**Source:** [https://fluentcommunity.co/docs/leaderboard-overview-and-management/](https://fluentcommunity.co/docs/leaderboard-overview-and-management/)

The Leaderboard in FluentCommunity is a feature that highlights and ranks members based on their engagement and activities. It helps foster competition and recognizes active participants, contributing to a more engaging and interactive community experience.

**Visibility:** To view the Leaderboard page click on the **Leaderboard** from the top navbar.

**Ranks & Titles:**Members are assigned ranks and titles based on their accumulated points. This gamification element encourages participation and engagement.

## Managing the Leaderboard

Go to the **Manage Leaderboard** section and then select the **Feature and Addons**from the left sidebar. Now you will see the Leaderboard Module. Click on the Settings along with the Leaderboard Module.

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/Leaderboard-1-scaled.webp)

A popup will appear with the checkbox option to enable the Leaderboard Module of FluentCommunity. Check the Enable Leaderboard Module checkbox.

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/Leaderboard-2.webp)

## Managing Ranks

In the Leaderboard Levels section, you can edit the rank **Title** and **Description**. Simply click on the respective fields and customize the **Title** and **Description** as needed.

**Minimum Points:** Alongside the Title and Description, you can assign minimum points to define the criteria for achieving a rank and progressing to the next level. Enter the desired points in the provided field along with the Title and Description.

> Members earn 1 point for each like they receive on their posts, articles, or comments.

Click on the **Save Settings** button to save all the changes you have made.

![](https://fluentcommunity.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/Leaderboard-3.webp)

By utilizing the Leaderboard feature in FluentCommunity, you can effectively manage member engagement, recognize top contributors, and foster a competitive and interactive environment within your community.
