## Fluent Forms Glossary

**Source:** [https://fluentforms.com/docs/fluent-forms-glossary/](https://fluentforms.com/docs/fluent-forms-glossary/)

Welcome to the Fluent Forms Glossary! This guide explains key terms and essential features to help you better understand Fluent Forms and its features.

**Form Builder**

The Form Builder is where you create and manage all your forms. Everything starts here, from simple contact forms to multi-step surveys, payment forms, quizzes, and registration forms.

**Fields**

Fields are the core parts of a form where users enter information. Fluent Forms offers many fields you can easily drag and drop into your form without coding.

In the **Free** version, you get basic fields like name, email, dropdowns, checkboxes, radio buttons, date pickers, and address fields.

The **Pro** version adds powerful options like file and image uploads, payment fields, repeaters, calculation fields, ratings, dynamic fields, and quiz or post-creation fields.

Fields are organized into **General** and **Advanced** groups, so you can quickly find what you need to build everything from a simple contact form to complex, multi-step forms with logic and payments.

**Integration**

Fluent Forms lets you connect your forms to tools you already use. You can send form data to **Mailchimp, Slack, Google Sheets, HubSpot, Salesforce, Twilio, Google Maps,** and many others.

It also works with payment gateways like **Stripe, Razorpay, and PayPal.** With 60+ integrations available, Fluent Forms makes automating tasks, collecting leads, and accepting payments through your forms easy.

**Settings**

You have complete control over how your forms behave. Customize messages, email notifications, and form schedules.

Payment settings let you choose currencies and payment types and manage gateways. Global settings allow you to apply default options across all forms, which saves time and keeps things consistent.

**AI** **Form** **Builder**

Create complete forms just by describing what you need. The **AI** Assistant in Fluent Forms uses your prompt to build the form instantly. this helps to save your valuable time. To know more about Fluent Form AI Builder read this [article](https://fluentforms.com/docs/how-to-create-a-form-with-fluent-forms-ai/) here.

**Entry**

An entry is the information saved when someone fills out and submits a form. You can view these entries in your WordPress dashboard and export them as **CSV**, **Excel**, or **JSON** files.

**Styling & Customization**

Customize how your forms look with built-in design options. You can adjust colors, fonts, borders, and layouts without **CSS**. Pro users also get extended styling features.

You can control every aspect of your form, from its design to how it functions. If you want advanced customization, you can add your **CSS** or **JavaScript**.

**Shortcode**

A shortcode is a tiny text you copy and paste into a page, post, or widget area. Just paste it where you want the form to appear.

**Widget**

Widgets let you place forms in sidebars, footers, or other widget-ready spots. You can also use Fluent Forms widgets like **Elementor** or **Oxygen** inside page builders.

**Import/Export**

Do you need to move a form to another site or keep a backup? Export it to a file and import it elsewhere. You can even import forms from plugins like WPForms or Contact Form 7.

**Webhook**

A webhook sends form data to another app or service once someone submits the form. It is excellent for custom workflows and automation, especially with CRM or marketing tools.

**Conditional Logic**

Conditional logic makes your form smart. Based on users’ choices, you can show or hide questions, send different emails, or display special messages.

**PDF Generator**

The PDF Generator turns form submissions into downloadable PDF files. It can be used for receipts, certificates, summaries, or other documents.

This glossary offers a general overview of Fluent Forms’ essential terminology and concepts.

## Fluent Forms User Interface

**Source:** [https://fluentforms.com/docs/fluent-forms-user-interface/](https://fluentforms.com/docs/fluent-forms-user-interface/)

Fluent Forms is the most powerful, user-friendly drag-and-drop form-building plugin for WordPress. It allows you to create, manage, and customize website forms.

This documentation walks you through the key elements and capabilities of the Fluent Forms user interface.

## Dashboard

The dashboard is Fluent Forms’ main control centre. From here, you can view an overview of all your forms, track recent submissions, view analytics, and manage everything in one place.

At the top, you’ll find menu items like **Entries**, **Payments**, **Global Settings**, **Tools**, **Integrations**, and **Support**, giving you quick access to all major sections.

- **Add New Form**: Click this button to start building a new form for your site.
- **Search Bar**: Located at the top-right, you can quickly search through your forms and form data.
- **Filter Options**: Easily filter form stats by day, week, month, or a custom date range to analyze performance.

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/filter-form-01-scaled.webp)

### Form List Overview

- **ID:**Displays the unique ID of each form.
- **Title:** Shows the name or title of your form.
- **Shortcode:**Each form has a shortcode that you can copy and paste anywhere on your site (pages, posts, or widgets) to display the form.
- **Entries:**View all submissions collected through each form, including recent entries.

## Form Menu

This menu helps you quickly manage and organize each form directly from the dashboard. When you hover over a specific form, a menu appears with several options to manage the form.

- **Edit**: Opens the form builder so you can change the form.
- **Settings**: This takes you to the form’s settings page.
- **Entries**: Shows all submissions made through that form.
- **Preview**: Let’s see how the form will appear on the front end.
- **Duplicate**: Creates a copy of the form with the same structure and settings.
- **Export**: You can export the form for backup or use it on another site.
- **Find**: Helps you locate similar forms.
- **Delete**: Permanently remove the form from your site.
- **Active/Inactive**: Use the toggle button to activate or deactivate the form on your website.

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/unnamed-3.webp)

## Forms Category

You can filter your forms by category using the drop-down menu. This helps you quickly find specific types of forms you’ve created on your website:

- **All**: Shows every form you’ve created.
- **Active**: Displays only the forms that are currently active.
- **Inactive**: Lists all the forms that are deactivated.
- **Payment Form**: Filters to show only forms that include payment options.
- **Conversational Form**: Shows forms designed in a chat-like, conversational style.
- **Multi-step Form**: Displays forms that are split into multiple pages or steps.

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/form-category-02-1-scaled.webp)

## Form Builder

The Form Builder allows you to create and customize forms using a drag-and-drop system.

- **Field Panel:**This is the sidebar that shows all available form fields, including general and advanced types like text input, checkboxes, and dropdowns. You can drag any field from here to your form.
- **Customization:**On the right side, you’ll find options to customize each field after adding it to the form.
- **Form Canvas:**This is the main workspace where you design and arrange your form.
- **Field Settings:**When you click on a field, you can edit its label, placeholder, validation rules, and other options.
- **Container:**Use containers to create multi-column layouts. You can also resize them as needed.
- **Preview:**Click the Preview button to see how your form will look on the front end.
- **Save Form:**After making changes, click the Save Form button to save your form to your website.

> If you do not have any existing forms, read Create a Form from Scratch or Create a Form using Templates documentation to create a new one.

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/Form-builder-scaled.webp)

### Form Template

You can use a collection of ready-made form templates to quickly get started.

- **Import Button:**Click this button to import an existing template into your form builder.
- **Choose a Template:**Pick and customize a pre-designed template to fit your needs.
- **Create Conversational Form:**Use this option to start building a conversational-style form.
- **Create a Post Form:**Select this template to create a form that can generate WordPress posts. To learn more about Post Forms, check the related [guide](https://fluentforms.com/docs/how-to-create-a-post-form-with-fluent-forms/).
- **Create a Form with AI:**Choose this option to generate a form using AI. To learn more about Fluent Forms AI Builder, read this[article](https://fluentforms.com/docs/how-to-create-a-form-with-fluent-forms-ai/).

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Form-template-04.webp)

### Entries

The Entries section shows all the form submissions collected on your website. You can filter them by specific forms or by read and unread status. You can also import entries from other sources. For more details, check out the full article on Entries.

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/entries-05-scaled.webp)

### Payment

Once you enable the payment feature, you can choose and set up your preferred payment methods, such as Stripe, PayPal, or Razorpay. You can also connect specific forms to specific payment options based on your needs.

When you click the **Payments** menu, you’ll see a list of all transactions made through your forms. You can view payment details for each form separately.

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Payment-07-scaled.webp)

### Global Settings

**Global Settings** is the section where you can manage both general and form-specific settings in one place. From here, you can adjust overall options like default layouts, integration settings, form behaviors, and other preferences that apply to all forms on your site.

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Settings-08-scaled.webp)

### Tools

The Tools section in the Fluent Forms Dashboard allows you to easily manage and transfer your forms and data. In this section, you can:

- View API activity logs
- Export and import forms
- Import entries from JSON or CSV files
- Migrate forms from other plugins.
- Monitor form activity

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/tools-09-scaled.webp)

## Integration

Here is the list of modules you can turn on or off using the **Toggle**button.

**Modules:**There are three fluent forms module options: **All**, **CRM & SASS integration,** and **WP Core module**. You can search for modules in the right-side search boxes.
**Fluent Form PDF:** The Fluent Forms PDF module allows users to generate and download form submissions as PDF files.

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Integration-09-scaled.webp)

### Support

This **Support** menu contains user guidance, documentation, community support, and expert live support. You can also request features here.

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/Support.webp)

This overview highlights the essential components of the Fluent Forms UI, making it easier for users to navigate and utilize the plugin effectively.

## How to Upgrade to Fluent Form Pro Add-on

Before upgrading to Fluent Forms Pro, ensure you have the **Pro Add-on .zip file** ready. Below is a step-by-step process to get the .zip file-

### Get the Zip File

You’ll first need to purchase the**Pro**version of Fluent Forms to get started. You can do this in two ways:

Visit the official[Fluent Forms](https://fluentforms.com/)****or[WPManageNinja](https://wpmanageninja.com/)****websites and choose a pricing plan that fits your needs.

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/Pricing-plan.webp)

Or, you can open the **Fluent Forms free**plugin from your WordPress dashboard and click the **Upgrade to Pro** button. This will take you directly to the purchase page.

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/01-2-scaled.webp)

Once your purchase is complete, you can access the WPManageninja Dashboard. Log in to the[account](https://wpmanageninja.com/account/dashboard/) with your credentials.

Then, go to the **Dashboard**, and you will find **Fluent Forms** under **Downloads**or**Purchase Story**. Now, click the Download button from the left sidebar, and you will see the Fluent Forms **.zip** file available for download to your device.

To access your Fluent Forms license, navigate to **Licenses** in the left sidebar. You’ll find your Fluent Forms license key there. You’ll need this key later when activating the plugin. If you miss it now, you can always come back and retrieve it from your WPManageNinja Dashboard.

## Install the Fluent Form Pro

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/02-3-scaled.webp)

Now, click the **Upload** **Plugin** button. Then, press the **Choose file** to upload the Fluent Forms .zip file you’ve downloaded before, and click the**Install Now** button.

The Fluent Forms Pro plugin will be successfully installed on your WordPress site!

> Do not deactivate or uninstall the Fluent Forms plugin from the Installed Plugins list. If you do this, you will be unable to use the Pro features and will need to reinstall and activate the Pro add-on.

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/04-3-scaled-04-25-2025_12_52_PM.webp)

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/activate-plugin-04-25-2025_12_37_PM.webp)

To activate all the Pro features of Fluent Forms with the license key, follow the steps with the screenshots below —

## How to Install Fluent Forms

**Source:** [https://fluentforms.com/docs/how-to-install-fluent-forms/](https://fluentforms.com/docs/how-to-install-fluent-forms/)

Are you ready to Install Fluent Forms on your WordPress Website? Before starting the installation process, let’s quickly check the requirements to ensure everything is in place.

### Requirements

To use Fluent Form, you need –

- WordPress version 4.5 or later
- PHP version 5.4 or later

### Installing Fluent Forms

Now, it’s time to get started with Fluent Forms. There are two ways to install Fluent Forms –

1. You can install Fluent Forms from your WordPress dashboard.
2. Alternatively, download the Fluent Forms from wordpress.org and manually install it on your WordPress dashboard.

We will cover both of these processes.

### Installing Fluent Forms from WordPress Dashboard

The steps of installing Fluent Forms are just like installing any other WordPress plugin. Here are the steps –

- Navigate to **WordPress Dashboard** **Plugins**section and click **Add New Plugin**.
- Now, search for **Fluent Forms.** Click on**Install Now**. Wait for the plugin to be installed, and then click **Activate** to activate it.

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/Fluent-Form-install-01-scaled.webp)

### Installing Fluent Forms from the WordPress Repository

You can manually install Fluent Forms by downloading it from the WordPress plugin repository. First, go to the [Fluent Forms](https://wordpress.org/plugins/fluentform/) page on WordPress.org and click the **Download** button to get the plugin file. Then, upload it to your WordPress site to complete the installation.

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/02-2-scaled.webp)

Now, navigate to your WordPress Dashboards **Plugin**sections. Click the **Add New**button from the left sidebar and then click the **Upload Plugin** button.

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/03-3-scaled.webp)

After that, click, **choose the .zip f**ile you just downloaded and click on the **Install Now** button.

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/04-2-scaled.webp)

Once the plugin is installed, search for **Fluent Forms** in the list of your installed plugins and click on **Activate**.

![](https://fluentforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/05-scaled.webp)

There you go! You have just installed the best WordPress form builder solution. Now, you are all set to explore all the impressive features it offers you.
