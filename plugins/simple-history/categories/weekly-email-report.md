## Weekly email reports

**Source:** [https://simple-history.com/support/weekly-email-report/](https://simple-history.com/support/weekly-email-report/)

To modify the recipients of the Simple History weekly email reports go to Simple History » Settings and add or remove email addresses.

Click save when you’re done and the next monday the recipients will get a fresh summary of the last week!
