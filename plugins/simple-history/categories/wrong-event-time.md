## ⏰ Why is the time of my events wrong?

**Source:** [https://simple-history.com/support/wrong-event-time/](https://simple-history.com/support/wrong-event-time/)

If the time shown in the log is some hours before or behind your computer time, please check your **timezone setting**:

- Go to WP Admin › Settings › General.
- Check if the “Local time is” is your current/wanted time. If not – set it to your timezone.
- Now events in the log should use correct times!
