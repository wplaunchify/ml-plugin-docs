## Got a charge from “LEMSQZY* SIMPLE-HISTORY”?

**Source:** [https://simple-history.com/support/got-a-charge-from-lemsqzy-simple-history/](https://simple-history.com/support/got-a-charge-from-lemsqzy-simple-history/)

Do you have a charge from Lemon Squeezy or LEMSQZY* SIMPLE-HISTORY and don’t know why?

It probably means that you or any other person with access to your card has bought any of [our add-ons](https://simple-history.com/add-ons/) .

Depending on your bank account and card issuer the charge can be shorter, for example: “LEMSQZY* SIMPLE-H” or “”LEMSQZY* SIMPLE-HI”.

You’ve probably noticed a charge from LEMSQZY* SIMPLE-HISTORY on your credit card or bank statement and not always understood why. We’ll clear up why you are seeing these charges and why it’s completely normal.
