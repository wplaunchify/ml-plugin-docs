## Email Sending Error Notification Discord

**Source:** [https://fluentsmtp.com/docs/email-sending-error-notification-discord/](https://fluentsmtp.com/docs/email-sending-error-notification-discord/)

FluentSMTP can send your Email Sending Error Notifications to Discord. Here are the step-by-step guidelines on how you can enable your Discord Notification.

Go to your FluentSMTP **Alert** and you will see the **Discord** click on it and a configuration page will appear.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/1.png)

Here you have to give your **Discord Channel**name in which channel you want to get a notification and then you have to give the **Webhook URL**. To get this Webhook URL go to your **Discord Account**.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/2.png)

Now in your Discord account go to the channel where you want to receive the notifications. On the right side of the Channel, you will find a **Settings**button. Click on the **Settings**button.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/3.png)

Select the **Integrations** from the **Left Sidebar**of your Discord Channel settings and then you will see the **Webhook**. Here you have to create a Webhook so click on the **Create Webhook** button.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/4.png)

Here you can give it any name if you want and then just copy the **Webhook URL**.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/5.png)

Now go back to your FluentSMTP account give the channel name the same as the Discord channel and paste your Discord Webhook URL here.

Click on the **Configure Discord Notification** button.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/6.png)

Your Discord configuration is complete now. You will get notifications for your Email Sending Errors now on your Discord channel.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/7.png)

## Email Summary

**Source:** [https://fluentsmtp.com/docs/alerts-email-summary/](https://fluentsmtp.com/docs/alerts-email-summary/)

The Summary Email feature provides detailed statistics on your email sending activities. Tailor this report to your preferences by selecting the specific day of the week for receiving email stats. Additionally, you have the flexibility to choose the email address to which these email report stats will be sent.

To activate the Summary Email feature, go to FluentSMTP Alert. Enable it by checking the designated checkbox.

By default, the Notification Email Addresses will be set to the admin email address, but you can customize it according to your preferences. Further, personalize your experience by choosing the days on which you want to receive the email stats.

Lastly, click on the Save Settings button to save your changes.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/FluentSMTP-‹-Perfume-Shop-—-WordPress-1-1.png)

## Email Sending Error Notification Telegram

**Source:** [https://fluentsmtp.com/docs/email-sending-error-notification-telegram/](https://fluentsmtp.com/docs/email-sending-error-notification-telegram/)

FluentSMTP will notify you **Email Sending Error Notification** for the emails you have been sent and you can get this notification on your **Telegram**.

This guide will show you how to activate your Telegram notification. Let’s start the notification activation process.

First, go to your FluentSMTP then navigate to the **Alerts**. You will see the **Telegram** here click on it and your Telegram notification configuring settings will be open.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/FluentSMTP-‹-Perfume-Shop-—-WordPress-2-1.png)

## Enable Telegram Notification

From the configure notification settings of Telegram, you will find the official **FluentSMTP Telegram Bot** just click on the **link** to add the Telegram Bot to your Telegram Account. You must have to log in to your Telegram account first.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/FluentSMTP-‹-Perfume-Shop-—-WordPress-3-1-1.png)

After clicking on the official Telegram bot link you will be redirected to the Telegram bot of FluentSMTP, here click on the **Send Message** button.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/1.6.png)

Now in your Telegram account, FluentSMTP Bot will arrive like this. Click on the **Start**button.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/1.7-709x1024.png)

Return to your FluentSMTP Telegram settings here agree on the terms and conditions of this Telegram Integration and click on the **Continue** button.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/FluentSMTP-‹-Perfume-Shop-—-WordPress-4-1.png)

Now you will get an **Activation PIN** code. Copy the PIN code.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/FluentSMTP-‹-Perfume-Shop-—-WordPress-5-1.png)

Paste the PIN code on your Telegram Account and send it to the Telegram FluentSMTP Bot and you will get a *Congratulations!* message from your FluentSMTP Telegram bot.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/7-702x1024.png)

Click on **I have sent the code** button.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/FluentSMTP-‹-Perfume-Shop-—-WordPress-10-1.png)

Now you are done! you will get your Email Sending Error Notifications on your Telegram.

The Telegram username is shown in the FluentSMTP Telegram settings. If you want to remove your Telegram account from FluentSMTP notifications click the **Disconnect** button.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/FluentSMTP-‹-Perfume-Shop-—-WordPress-11-1.png)

Congrats! You have successfully added your Telegram account in FluentSMTP for Email sending error notifications.

## Email Sending Error Notification Slack

**Source:** [https://fluentsmtp.com/docs/email-sending-error-notification-slack/](https://fluentsmtp.com/docs/email-sending-error-notification-slack/)

To Enable **Email Sending Error Notifications** in your **Slack** Account go to your FluentSMTP **Alerts**. Now click on **Slack**to go to the notification settings.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/FluentSMTP-‹-Perfume-Shop-—-WordPress-6-1.png)

Agree to the Terms and Conditions and click on **Continue** **to Slack**.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/FluentSMTP-‹-Perfume-Shop-—-WordPress-7-1.png)

Now you will be redirected to **Slack**. You have to log in to your **Slack Account**. Now select the **Workspace** and the **Channel**where you want to get the **Email**error notification****and click****the **Allow** button.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/10.png)

After that, you will see your Slack **Workspace** and **Channel** name in the FluentSMTP Slack settings. If you want to disconnect Slack just click on the **Disconnect**button.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/FluentSMTP-‹-Perfume-Shop-—-WordPress-8-1.png)

In your Slack account, you will see your email error notifications like this.

![](https://fluentsmtp.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/12.png)

That’s it! This is how you can connect your Slack with FluentSMTP for Email error notification. If you have any queries feel free to contact us.
