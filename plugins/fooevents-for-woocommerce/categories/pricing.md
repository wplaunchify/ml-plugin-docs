				## Frequently Asked Questions
				Who is FooEvents for?
					Anyone with a WooCommerce-enabled WordPress website who needs to sell tickets, slots, sessions, admissions or passes should use FooEvents! It is the fastest, simplest way to add robust ticket selling capabilities to your WordPress website.
							Can FooEvents be used for large events?
					Absolutely. FooEvents has been used by over 10,000 events, some of which have sold over 75,000 tickets.
							What languages does FooEvents support?
					The FooEvents plugins come bundled with language files for *Afrikaans*, *Catalan*, *Czech*, *German*, *Spanish*, *French*, *Italian*, *Dutch* and *Portuguese*. These translations are available if your WordPress site is set to any of these languages or locales. You can also create your own [custom language files](https://help.fooevents.com/docs/topics/translations/language-files/).
							Where can I find a demo of FooEvents?
					We’ve curated an extensive collection of working [front-end demos](https://demo.fooevents.com/) that showcase FooEvents in a wide range of use cases.

If you would like to test out all the latest FooEvents plugins from within the WordPress back-end, please visit the [admin demo](https://fooevents.com/24-hour-demo/) page to instantly activate a 24 hour sandbox demo which can be extended by [contacting us](https://help.fooevents.com/contact/) if required.

[FooEvents POS](https://www.fooevents.com/products/fooevents-pos/) has its own 24 hour demo which can be [activated here](https://pos.fooevents.com/).
							Do you offer a free trial?
					We don’t offer a free trial but we do offer a [14-Day No Quibble Money-Back Guarantee](https://www.fooevents.com/refunds/), so you can buy with total confidence.
							Is FooEvents a once-off purchase?
					A subscription entitles you to 12 months of support and updates from the date of purchase. You may cancel your subscription at any time by logging into your [FooEvents Account](https://www.fooevents.com/my-account/), however, you must have a valid subscription or purchase a new one in order to download the latest plugin updates and receive technical support.
							What payment methods do you accept?
					FooEvents orders are processed securely in US dollars (USD) by our international payment partner [Stripe](https://www.stripe.com/) (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, Diners Club and JCB are accepted).

South African customers will be charged in South Africa rand (ZAR) and payments will be processed securely by [Paystack](https://www.paystack.com/) (Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Verve are accepted).

[PayPal](https://www.paypal.com/) is also a payment option for all countries except for South Africa and Canada.
							Can the FooEvents plugins be purchased separately?
					Yes, the FooEvents for WooCommerce core plugin and the FooEvents extensions can either be purchased as part of a bundle or separately from their respective product pages.
							Why isn't the FooEvents Bookings plugin included in the Premium Bundle?
					One of the reasons why the FooEvents Bookings plugin is not included in the FooEvents Premium bundle is to keep the cost of the FooEvents Premium bundle as low as possible. The booking slot functionality in the FooEvents Bookings plugin is an extra feature with added value, since the core FooEvents for WooCommerce plugin and FooEvents Multi-day plugin extension have always only focused on events where all attendees attend on the same date(s) e.g. a concert, multi-day conference etc.

Another reason why they are not currently part of the same bundle is because the FooEvents Bookings and FooEvents Multi-day plugins are not specifically integrated with each other since they are intended to be used in different ways. The FooEvents Multi-day plugin is better suited to events that span over multiple days and where **all** attendees are expected to attend **all** the days. It does not let your customers choose a date, but can work well if you are selling passes since it allows check-ins on more than one day/session. The FooEvents Bookings plugin lets your customers **choose** a specific date and/or time slot.
							Do I need to install other FooEvents plugins to use FooEvents POS?
					FooEvents POS is a standalone plugin which means that you don’t need to install any other FooEvents plugin extensions in order to use the standard features of the FooEvents POS app. However, in order to use any of the FooEvents integration features including selling and printing tickets, making bookings and selecting seats, you will need to purchase, install and activate the respective FooEvents plugins that perform this functionality, namely, [FooEvents for WooCommerce](https://www.fooevents.com/products/fooevents-for-woocommerce/) (core), [FooEvents Multi-day](https://www.fooevents.com/products/fooevents-multi-day/), [FooEvents Bookings](https://www.fooevents.com/products/fooevents-bookings/) and [FooEvents Seating](https://www.fooevents.com/products/fooevents-seating/).

All these plugins can be purchased separately or as part of a discounted [FooEvents Point of Sale Bundle](https://www.fooevents.com/bundles/pos-bundle/).
							Can I upgrade/downgrade my FooEvents License?
					Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade an active FooEvents License at any time by logging into your [FooEvents Account](https://www.fooevents.com/my-account/).
							What are the requirements for FooEvents?
					FooEvents requires [WordPress](https://wordpress.org/) and [WooCommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/) to be installed.
							Do I need to know how to code to use FooEvents?
					No technical skills are required to use FooEvents! FooEvents is easy to install and manage from your WordPress Admin Area.
							Do you offer technical support if I need help?
					Yes, our support agents are distributed across different timezones and are always ready to help! Submit a [support ticket](https://help.fooevents.com/contact/) if you are experiencing a technical problem and we’ll be happy to assist.
							Can I use FooEvents for client web sites?
					Yes, we have created different licensing options which includes multiple and unlimited domains that web agencies and freelancers can purchase in order to implement FooEvents across multiple client sites.
