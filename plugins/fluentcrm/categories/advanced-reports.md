## Abandoned Cart Report

**Source:** [https://fluentcrm.com/docs/abandoned-cart-report/](https://fluentcrm.com/docs/abandoned-cart-report/)

The FluentCRM [Abandoned Cart](https://fluentcrm.com/what-is-abandoned-cart/) feature provides a detailed report on abandoned carts, including revenue generated and customer details. This report focuses solely on WooCommerce cart data.

You can use the **Date Filter** option to refine the report based on specific time frames.

![abandoned report 1](https://fluentcrm.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/Abandoned-Report-1-scaled.webp)

Scroll down to view the details for individual customers. Click the **Eye** icon button to see more information about that user’s cart.

## WooCommerce advanced reports

From the FluentCRM Report, navigate the Woocommerce then you’ll see the WooCommerce Advanced Reports.

FluentCRM provides detailed reports for your WooCommerce data.  You can see the following reports:

- **Total Revenue:** It shows all the money you make from your store selling products.
- **Total Order:** It presents your Store’s final order numbers.
- **Total customer:** It represents the number of people buying your products.
- **Average revenue per customer:** It figures out average sales per customer.
- **Average order value(AOV):**The average amount a customer spends on each purchase in your Store.
- **Average order Customer(AOC):**The average order spend per customer.
- **Top-selling product reports:** Best-selling products in your store. It is also viewable with a custom date range.

![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXda1xff51YdhHsWU0I3Nst1-aNyPSMfwsQYYbj6hzCpfz1xCxd5fVGR9GtTXrjRX2TZ4P02hlUrdWIrQL8R2AOCnWaicUBPwdbjliY7ApGb43WDVp0E1BGX3mlqEv0iqvgkhHUUNLiyA6sjIV5q1ATXhLI?key=e98Jl9JA0oMgYDMCowU4Jw)

> To display your WooCommerce data you must integrate it with WooCommerce plugins to FluentCRM. Learn more.

### Gross Volume

**Gross volume** represents total sales volume before any deductions. It can be displayed for specific products or all WooCommerce products sold. There is also a **date filter** to view more precise reports.

Below, you’ll find detailed statistics for your WooCommerce reports.

### Customer Growth

Here, you can see**Customer Growth**, which shows your business’s Total customer Volume. Analyze your customer valuation with a **date filter** that measures specific time periods.

![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXc9v7qJynRXUaOeGKHZfWoX5eJQ-74wl4n7tjp44zekT3tDBnCcbN4bnugWycbEUHHDzaTkDoV0s8Wmx0IKfoNI0nxFH6qNPAD6EnRXsD2dy-k0iAl_oC9jPpim0djOU_5c6-UKumecxRuosjQ1tgBWQeY?key=e98Jl9JA0oMgYDMCowU4Jw)

## LifterLMS Advanced reports

If you go to LifterLMS from the **Reports** option in FluentCRM, you can see an advanced report of the LifterLMS data on your site.

FluentCRM provides detailed reports for your LifterLMS data. You can track things like:

- **Total Students:**Overall number of unique persons who have ever enrolled in any course.
- **Total Course Enrollment:**Overall, several unique persons have ever enrolled in any course.
- **Total Membership Enrollment:**Total number of Membership enrollments.
- **Top-selling products:** Viewable by custom date change.

## EDD – Advanced Reports

**Source:** [https://fluentcrm.com/docs/edd-advanced-reports/](https://fluentcrm.com/docs/edd-advanced-reports/)

From the Reports option in FluentCRM, if you navigate to EDD then you can see an advanced report of the EDD data on your site.

The report data shows the **Total Revenue**, **Total Orders**, **Total Customers**, **Average Revenue Per Customer**, **Active Subscriptions**, **Cancelled Subscriptions**, **Average Order Value** (AOV), **Average Order/Customer** (AOC), and the **Top selling products** on your site.

You can also see a graphical report of the **Gross Volume**, **Customer Growth,** and **Subscriptions** over a certain period of time. You can also compare the report with a previous period.
