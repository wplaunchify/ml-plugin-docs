## FluentCart Integration With FluentBooking

**Source:** [https://fluentbooking.com/docs/fluentcart-integration-with-fluentbooking/](https://fluentbooking.com/docs/fluentcart-integration-with-fluentbooking/)

**FluentCart** is built for speed, scalability, and flexibility, helping you run your eCommerce business smoothly and efficiently. With this integration, you can sell consultations, classes, services, or products directly through your booking forms without requiring third-party plugins.

This keeps your sales and appointments within a single, seamless ecosystem without needing extra plugins.****Follow these steps to connect FluentCart with your booking events:

## Activate the FluentCart Module

First, ensure FluentCart is installed and active on your WordPress site.

Log in to your WordPress dashboard. Navigate to **FluentBooking** and select **Settings**from the top menu bar.

Next, click on **“Advanced Features & Addons.”** Here, you will see the system automatically enabled once you install and activate the FluentCart plugin.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/activate-the-fluentcart-module-scaled.webp)

## Configure FluentCart Inside Your Booking Event

Once the module is active, you need to connect it to a specific calendar event.

Go to **Booking Calendars** from your FluentBooking dashboard. Click the **Edit** button on the specific event you want to monetize. If you haven’t created a booking event yet, click on the **+ New** button.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/edit-calendar-02-scaled.webp)

### Set Up the Product and Checkout

From the left sidebar menu, select **Payment Settings**. Then, check the box to enable **“Enable this payment as Paid and collect payment on booking**”.

Under **Payment Method**, select **FluentCart Checkout** from the dropdown menu.

**Select a Product:** You will see a dropdown list of your existing FluentCart products. Select the appropriate product for this service.

> Note: If you don’t have a product yet, click + New to create one directly from this screen.
> 
> 
> 
> Free Version Note: Users on the free version can connect to only one product.

**Multiple Durations (Optional):** If you have set up multiple meeting durations for your event, you can assign different products and prices to specific durations. Click the **Save Changes** button to finalize the setup.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/payment-settings-03-scaled.webp)

### Booking Order and Payment Confirmation

Once configured, your invitees will see a price displayed on your booking form that matches the connected FluentCart product.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/event-4.webp)

**The User Experience:** When an invitee selects a time and proceeds, the entire payment flow is handled via the **FluentCart checkout page**. This ensures a secure and familiar payment experience for your clients.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/checkout-5.webp)

**Order Completion:** After the order is completed, the booking is fully processed. The payment is recorded via the FluentCart method you configured.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/receipt-6.webp)

### Managing Bookings and Orders

All bookings made through this integration are automatically synced between the two plugins.

**In FluentBooking,** you can view the appointment details in the **Bookings** section.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/booking-details-6-7-scaled.webp)

**In FluentCart:** Each booking is recorded as an official **Order**, ensuring your sales data and customer records remain centralized in one ecosystem.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/order-details-7-scaled.webp)

**Congratulations!** You have successfully integrated FluentCart with FluentBooking, turning your appointments into a fully connected WooCommerce-powered sales channel.

If you have any further questions, concerns, or suggestions, please do not hesitate to contact our[support team](https://wpmanageninja.com/account/dashboard/). Thank you.

## FluentBoards Integration with FluentBooking

**Source:** [https://fluentbooking.com/docs/fluentboards-integration-with-fluentbooking/](https://fluentbooking.com/docs/fluentboards-integration-with-fluentbooking/)

FluentBoards integrates with FluentBooking, allowing you to create a task before booking any event. Install and activate the FluentBoards plugin. Follow the simple step-by-step process to integrate FluentBoards with FluentBooking.

## Integrate with FluentBooking

Go to the specific booking event where you want to integrate with FluentBoards. From the Fluentbooking dashboard, click the **Calendars** menu from the top navbar. Next, click the **Edit** icon.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/select-calendar-01-scaled.webp)

Here appears a new page. Now select the **Integrations** section from the left side bar. After that, click the **Add New Integration** button and choose the**FluentBoards Integrations** option.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/Add-new-integration-02-scaled.webp)

## Configure FluentBoards Integration Feed

Now, here you need to map your FluentBoards Integration Feed to add a Task from your FluentBooking to FluentBoard.

**A. Feed Name:** Give a suitable feed name here.

**B. FluentBoards Configuration:** In the FluentBoards configuration fields, you can select Board, Stage, Labels, Assignees, and Priority from the dropdown list.

**C. Task Title:** Enter a task title that shows on your task board. If you want to add dynamic information, you can use shortcodes by clicking the **three-dot** menu next to the field.

**D. Description:**Description will be added as Task Description.

**E. Submitter Name:**Enter a name that shows the task submitter’s name. You can use a shortcode to add the submitter’s email.

**F. Submitter Email:** Enter submitter email here.

**G. Due Type:** Select the due type, Booking date or Meeting date.

**H. Due Date:** Set a due date for the task using the Plus (+) or Minus (-) icons to adjust the date as needed.

**I. Task Position:** You can select the Task Position in the stage from here.

**J. Event Trigger:** Select in which booking stage you want to trigger this feed by checking the checkbox.

**K. Status:**Enable this feed to create a task for this booking event.

After everything is done, click on the **Save Feed** button to save this FluentBoards Integration Feed.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/unnamed-1-05-12-2025_10_45_AM.webp)

Your task will be automatically generated upon form submission and placed into the board and stage you’ve chosen.

You can easily disable, enable, edit, and delete the task by clicking the icon.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/unnamed.webp)

## Viewing the Boards in FluentBoards

To see your boards in FluentBoards, simply go to the FluentBoards **Dashboard**. From there, click on the task for the board you want to view. You’ll find all the boards assigned from **FluentBooking** right in one place.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/boards-scaled.webp)

If you have any further questions, concerns, or suggestions, please do not hesitate to contact our [support team](https://wpmanageninja.com/support-tickets/?utm_source=wpmn&utm_medium=home&utm_campaign=site#/). Thank you.

## Nextcloud Calendar Integration With FluentBooking

**Source:** [https://fluentbooking.com/docs/next-cloud-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/](https://fluentbooking.com/docs/next-cloud-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/)

FluentBooking easily connects with Nextcloud Calendar, so you can sync your booking events hassle-free. Just follow a few simple steps to integrate FluentBooking with Nextcloud Calendar.

## Enabling Nextcloud Calendar

To enable Nextcloud Calendar go to your FluentBooking **Settings** and here you will find your **Nextcloud Calendar** settings in the left sidebar. Now click on the Checkbox to enable the **Nextcloud Calendar.**Also, you have to give here the **Nextcloud CalDav Primary URL**.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/0.png)

### Nextcloud CalDav Primary URL

Login to your **Nextcloud Developer Account** to get the **Primary CalDav URL**. Now select **Calendar** from your Nextcloud account and click on the **Calendar Settings**.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/1-1.png)

Now Scroll down to get the **CalDav Primary URL** and copy the **URL** from here.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/2-1.png)

Paste the **URL** on your FluentBooking **Nextcloud Calendar Settings** then click on the **Save Settings** button like the below screenshot.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/3-1.png)

## Nextcloud Calendar for Host

After enabling your Nextcloud Calendar, you need to add your Nextcloud Calendar to your **Host** profile.

Go to your **Host settings** of the host for whom you want to add the Nextcloud Calendar.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/host-1.png)

Now select the **Remote Calendar** from the left sidebar and select the **Connect with Nextcloud Calendar** button.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/host-2.png)

You will need the**Nextcloud ID (Username/Email)**and an **App Specific Password** here. To get these credentials you need to go to your Nextcloud Account again.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/host-3.png)

### Nextcloud Calendar App Credentials

Login to your [Nextcloud Account](https://nextcloud.com/sign-up/) and go to your**Nextcloud Profile Settings.**

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/host-4.png)

Select the **Security** from the left sidebar.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/host-5.png)

Scroll down and go to the **Devices & Sessions** section, you have to create an **App** here. Give the **App** name and click on the **Create new app password** button.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/host-6.png)

You will get your **Nextcloud ID (Username/Email)** and **App Specific Password**here. Copy these credentials and click on the **Done**button.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/host-7.png)

Paste the credentials to your FluentBooking host’s Remote Calendar settings and click on the **Connect with Nextcloud Calendar** button.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/host-8.png)

Now select the Nextcloud calendar to add the booking events on the selected calendar.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/host-9.png)

And that’s it. Your FluentBooking integration with Nextcloud Calendar is done.

## Booking Event Preview on Nextcloud Calendar

In your Nextcloud Calendar, you’ll be shown your booking events like this.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/host-10.png)

If you have any queries about integrating FluentBooking with Nextcloud Calendar feel free to contact us.

## Twilio/SMS/WhatsApp Integration with FluentBooking

**Source:** [https://fluentbooking.com/docs/twilio-integration-with-fluentbooking/](https://fluentbooking.com/docs/twilio-integration-with-fluentbooking/)

[Twilio](https://www.twilio.com/en-us) is a powerful communication platform that allows messaging and other communication methods into their systems and applications. FluentBooking integrates with Twilio to send notifications via SMS/WhatsApp for booking events.

This guide explains how to integrate Twilio with FluentBooking to send event notifications via SMS and WhatsApp.

## Configure FluentBooking Integration with Twilio/SMS

To begin integrating Twilio with FluentBooking, follow these steps:

Start by navigating to the FluentBooking **Settings** and look for the **SMS** **by Twilio** where you can configure Twilio integration.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/new-1.png)

## Retrieve Twilio Credentials

To obtain the necessary credentials from Twilio, go to your [Twilio account](https://www.twilio.com/login).

On your Twilio account’s homepage, you will find the Twilio Number and the credentials you need. Copy the following information from the **Account Info** section: **Account SID**, **Auth Token**, and **My Twilio phone number**.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/1-4.png)

Paste the copied credentials into the designated fields on the FluentBooking Twilio configuration page. Afterward, click the **Save Settings** button to store this information.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/New-2.png)

## Setting Up WhatsApp Sandbox for FluentBooking

To receive notifications via WhatsApp you have set up WhatsApp sandbox in Twilio. Follow the below steps to complete the process.

### Connect to Sandbox

In the left-hand sidebar of the Twilio Console, click on **Messaging**. Select **Send a WhatsApp Message** to access the WhatsApp Sandbox setup.

Twilio will provide you with a **WhatsApp number and a code**. These are necessary for sandbox activation.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/1-6.png)

Create a new contact on your personal WhatsApp account using the provided WhatsApp number and send the code you got from Twilio.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/2-6.png)

### Business Initiated Message

Now you can send your business template notification message to your WhatsApp number here.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/3-7.png)

### User-Initiated Conversation

In this step, reply to the notification from Twilio, which will appear as another **Message** notification in the Twilio WhatsApp Sandbox.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/4-5.png)

### Wrapping Up

Once you have completed the previous steps, click **Next step**. You will be guided to a completion page, confirming that the configuration of your WhatsApp Sandbox is done.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/5-3.png)

By following these steps, you will have successfully set up the WhatsApp Sandbox.

## Setting Up SMS Notifications via Twilio

Now that you have integrated Twilio, you can set up SMS/WhatsApp notifications for your events. Visit the FluentBooking **Dashboard** and go to the **Calendars**.

You have the flexibility to change the SMS/WhatsApp notification settings for existing bookings or create new event types.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/3-6.png)

You can personalize your SMS/WhatsApp notifications by specifying when to send them and editing the content.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/4-4.png)

If desired, you can select the default **Host Number**or you can give a **Custom Number** to receive notifications and create custom SMS messages. Also, you can include various shortcodes for additional customization, click on the three-dot button to get the shortcodes. Select the **Send**option that you want to receive notification via SMS or WhatsApp and then click the **Save SMS** button.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/new-3-1.png)

With these simple steps, you’ve successfully integrated Twilio into FluentBooking and configured SMS/WhatsApp notifications for your events. If you have any further questions or need assistance with Twilio Integration, please don’t hesitate to [contact us](https://wpmanageninja.com/account/dashboard/).

## FluentCRM integration with FluentBooking

**Source:** [https://fluentbooking.com/docs/fluentcrm-integration-with-fluentbooking/](https://fluentbooking.com/docs/fluentcrm-integration-with-fluentbooking/)

[FluentCRM](https://fluentcrm.com/)is a WordPress email marketing automation plugin that streamlines lead management, email campaigns, automatic sequences, learner and affiliate handling, and user activity tracking.

This integration between FluentCRM and FluentBooking simplifies the process of adding event attendees to your FluentCRM customer list for business growth and marketing lead collection.

## Integration Steps

Follow these straightforward steps to integrate FluentCRM with FluentBooking:

Access FluentBooking:

- Head to your FluentBooking **dashboard**.
- Navigate to **Booking Calendars** to find your booking events.
- Select the specific event you want to integrate with FluentCRM and click **Edit**.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Calendars-FluentBooking-18.png)

## Set Up FluentCRM Integration

On the event edit page, go to the left sidebar and select **Integrations**. Now click on **Add New Integration** and choose **FluentCRM Integration**

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/3-2.png)

### FluentCRM Integration Feeds

Customize your integration feed with the following options:

- **Feed Name:**Set or update your feed name.
- **Map Primary Fields:**Collect data from booking fields and you can use shortcodes for additional guest information.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Integrations-FluentBooking-10-13-2025_12_58_PM.webp)

- **Other Fields:** Add labels to store field values and you can use shortcodes to add more info here.
- **FluentCRM Lists:** Select the lists for your invitees.
- **Contact Tags:** Assign tags to attendees.

You’ll find some dynamic checkbox options here to further customize your invitees for FluentCRM customer lists.

- **Event Triggers:** Activate this Event Feed based on selected triggers. The integration will run whenever a booking’s status changes to one of the checked options. You can choose from: **Booking Confirmed**, **Booking** **Completed**, **Booking** **Canceled**, **Booking** **Rescheduled**, and **Booking Rejected**.
- **Remove Contact Tag:** You can also remove specific tags from the contact when this trigger is fired. Simply select the tags you wish to remove from the dropdown menu.
- **Status:** Select the **Enable this Feed** checkbox to activate this integration.

After completing your customization, click **Save Feed** to save your changes.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Integrations-FluentBooking-10-13-2025_01_11_PM-scaled.webp)

That’s all for FluentCRM Integration with FluentBooking. You can also see the attendee’s FluentCRM data inside the **Booking details**.

![booking details](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Booking-Details-1-1024x963.png)

Your booking details will be also shown in the attendee’s **FluentCRM** profile.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/photo_2023-10-17-12-13-51.png)

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out to our [support](https://wpmanageninja.com/account/dashboard/).

## Webhook Integration with FluentBooking

**Source:** [https://fluentbooking.com/docs/webhook-integration-with-fluentbooking/](https://fluentbooking.com/docs/webhook-integration-with-fluentbooking/)

FluentBooking’s robust webhook features allow you to easily share your booking events with any web API endpoint. As a result, your FluentBooking experience will be more flexible and versatile since you can easily exchange and integrate your booking data with various platforms.

## Steps to Configure Webhook

Select the **Booking Calendars** from the FluentBooking dashboard. Go to the particular booking event, then click the **Edit**button.

![](https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/noForieM7Wche_d4dIB8Dg_MxMdBYQYbvjWqOr3dhRMifs_ExReWwrFR6n46-BMr88YHX2Sjiv31ppzLU55NcPpTcAQeBoWJyfm4ZvsX8xuUaljmbrDyYvYL3PRuYa3ZqqQoGPYxnk0reeFXg4f9ZNQ)

On the left sidebar, you will see the **Webhook Feeds** for this particular booking. Select it.

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/Sh5-OmGBFUEUwrYhwH55dFLd8_-1Fws0GZho-OZka7k27K1RpVCS16C0qD_0GQlWtjkAlXwXFKw0-9vANsn-EV7rDP4AbsmnO0qCCOe_rwnxjUVPQIyc_Wwv5zm7s6Z0l5eR5_hLb_70g1pgZ_oQg_Q)

Now click on the **Add New Webhook** button

![](https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/5Ark8vrkOH8EPF25rZBNahozj6dcp3I9iyMuLA3QG48dWoXNyMRB93Ub62V2Oav1Sn0AKHoRhGeb5xWq_Q2ztyWHo0h_K3NKnMifnow26JfFl0zjhMpMK2NQq7SH4TFfJVJwr8GJSoi0oNRCgku7c5U)

## Configure the Webhook

**Name:** Enter a feed name to identify this setup uniquely.

**Request URL:** Enter the URL to be used in the Webhook request (the Webhook recipient URL).

**Request Method:** Select the HTTP method used for the webhook request. Choose from GET, POST, PUT, PATCH, or DELETE here.

**Request Format:** Select the format for the webhook request. Select between **FORM** and **JSON** from the drop-down menu.

**Request Header:** Select if any headers should be sent with the webhook request.

**Request Body:** Select if all fields or select fields should be sent with the webhook request.

**Event Triggers:**Choose the event triggers that best suit your needs. You have the flexibility to select from the options *Booking Confirmed*, *Booking Canceled, and Booking Completed* based on your specific requirements.

Finally, check “**Enable this webhook feed**” and click on **Save Feed** when the configuration is done.

## Fluent Forms Integration with FluentBooking

**Source:** [https://fluentbooking.com/docs/fluent-form-integration-with-fluentbooking/](https://fluentbooking.com/docs/fluent-form-integration-with-fluentbooking/)

With the simple integration of FluentBooking with Fluent Forms, you can easily add booking events to your forms. Here is a simple instruction for accomplishing that:

## Add Booking to the Fluent Forms

Open the **Fluent Form**s to get started. You have two options for adding your booking: either **Create a new form** or **Select an existing** one where you want to add your booking event.

Add the **FluentBooking Field** in the form from **Advanced Input** fields or click on the Plus icon button and search for FluentBooking. Add this input box by dragging it into your form or simply clicking it. Your booking field will be added to the form right away.

> Please note that, in order to take booking, you must add Name and Email field to that form.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/adding-Fluent-Booking-02-scaled.webp)

## Map with your specific calendar

Once you add the action hook field, you can customize it by hovering over it and clicking the **Pencil Icon.** It will take you to the **Input Customization** tab on the right side and offer the following options for customizing the field.

- FluentBooking Field
- Advanced Options

### FluentBooking Field

You will find various settings options for customizing the field. Located in the **Input Customization Tab**, these options allow you to configure essential aspects of the**FluentBooking**input field.

**All the Settings under the FluentBooking Field mentioned above are briefly explained below –**

**A. Element Label:**This is the field title users will see when filling out the **FluentBooking**field in a form. You can enter any text here to help users understand the field’s purpose.

**B. Admin Field Label:**The admin field label is a field title displayed only to admin users. You can configure this option to show a different field label for your admin users when they view the form submissions.

**C. Select Calendar:**Choose a calendar from the dropdown. The calendar must be created using the **FluentBooking** plugin.

**D. Select Guest Email Field:**Map the email field to ensure proper data collection.

**E. Select Guest Name Field:**Map the **name** field for user identification.

**F. Show Host Info:**If you want to show your host info, then check the **Show Host Info** option. If you do not want to show off your host info, then uncheck the**Show Host Info** option.

**G. Required:**Choose the appropriate option from here to determine whether the field should be empty when the user submits your form.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Fluent-booking-options-04-scaled.webp)

### Advanced Options

Advanced settings allow further customization to meet specific use cases. All the **Settings** under the **Advanced Options**tab mentioned in the screenshot are briefly explained below –

**A. Container Class:** Use this option to add your custom CSS classes to the field’s wrapper.

**B. Name Attribute:** The input field’s name attribute is the HTML equivalent of the same name. You don’t need to modify this.

**C. Conditional Logic:** This option allows you to create specific rules to dynamically hide/show the input field to function conditionally based on your submission values according to your set of conditional logic.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Advanced-options-05-scaled.webp)

## Preview of Added FluentBooking Field

Here is a preview of the form with the **FluentBooking** field.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Preview-07-scaled.webp)

That’s it! You’ve successfully added your booking event to Fluent Forms. Now, you will be able to see the form data inside the **Booking details** page and also from the form entries of Fluent Forms.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/FF-FB.png)

Please get in touch with us if you need any help integrating FluentBooking with Fluent Forms or if you have any issues. We’re [here](https://wpmanageninja.com/account/dashboard/) to assist you!

## Zoom Integration with FluentBooking

**Source:** [https://fluentbooking.com/docs/zoom-integration-with-fluentbooking/](https://fluentbooking.com/docs/zoom-integration-with-fluentbooking/)

Zoom integration simplifies the process of scheduling and handling online meetings, offering audio, chat, and video call options.

To integrate Zoom with FluentBoooking you need to follow some straightforward steps described in this guideline.

## Setting Up Zoom Integration

To connect your FluentBooking with Zoom you need some credentials. First, go to your[Zoom Marketplace](https://marketplace.zoom.us/) and log in or create a new account.

## Create Zoom App

You have to create a Zoom App in order to get the credentials for the integration. On the top right side, you will find the **Develop** button hover over the button and you will find the **Build App** option click on it.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/Zoom-app-building-scaled.webp)

Here you need to select the app type **Server-to-Sever OAuth App** then click on the **Create** button.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/zoom-server-selection-scaled.webp)

Now give a name to your App then click on the **Create** button.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/Zoom-app-name--scaled.webp)

### App Credentials

Now you will be taken to the **App** **Credential** page where you will get the App credentials. At this time you can **copy the credentials**to **FluentBooking** but that doesn’t complete the process, so**keep the copied credential in the clipboard for later use**.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/3-4.png)

### Information

On the Information page, there are some mandatory fields those are **Basic Information** Fields **App Name**, **Short Description**, **Company Name**, and **Developer Contact Information (****Name, Email****)**. You have to give this information and then click on the continue button.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/4-3.png)

### Feature

In the feature section, you don’t need to change anything so you can simply skip the feature section.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Feature-.png)

### Scopes

Here you have to add four scopes. Click on the Add Scopes button and a pop-up will come up.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/App-Marketplac.webp)

Select the following scopes from the **Meeting** and**User**Scopes then click on the **Done** button and continue:

From **Meeting** go to **View and manage all user meetings** and select these three scopes:

- **Delete a Meeting (meeting:delete:meeting:admin)**
- **Update a Meeting (meeting:update:meeting:admin)**
- **Create a meeting for a user (meeting:write:meeting:admin)**

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/Zoom-scope-adding-2-scaled.webp)

From **User** go to **View all User** **information** then select ‘**View a user (user:read:user:admin)’.**

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/Zoom-Scope-adding--scaled.webp)

### Activation

Once you have completed the above steps the Activation step will come. Simply click on the **Activate your app** button. This completes the process of creating a Zoom app.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/6.png)

## Configuring Zoom With FluentBooking

After creating the Zoom app properly now you can connect your Zoom with FluentBooking. Go to FluentBooking Dashboard and navigate to **Settings > Zoom** and you will see the button **Connect Your Zoom Account**click on the button.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/7.png)

Now a popup will appear paste your **copied** Zoom App **credentials** here and click on **Save & Validate Credentials.**

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/8.png)

FluentBooking and Zoom are successfully connected, you can see the connected host here. If you want to connect another user account of FluentBooking with Zoom click the **Add New User Account** button.

To connect another user account of FluentBooking with Zoom you need to create another app from another Zoom account and then repeat the same process to connect with Zoom.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/9.png)

## Zoom Integration From Host Setting

You can also set up Zoom from your host settings. Go to **Booking Calendars** of FluentBooking and click the **Host Settings** button on the host/calendar’s profile.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Calendars-FluentBooking-1-3.png)

Now go to the **Zoom Integration** and press the **Connect with Zoom Account**button.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Host-setting.png)

Now a popup will appear in the same way for your Zoom App **credentials** here paste your **copied**credentials here and click on **Save & Validate Credentials.**

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Host-settings-2.png)

## Add Zoom to the Event

Now at the **Event** **Location**, you will find the Zoom video for your meeting.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Slot-Settings-FluentBooking-15-1.png)

The host of the Fluentbooking and the attendees will receive the **Zoom video link** with the notification **mail**. Also, you will see your meetings in your Zoom account just like the screenshot below:

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/4d470267-0140-4a15-b0c1-c2686af946b7.png)

If you have any further queries about this integration feel free to reach our[support team](https://wpmanageninja.com/account/dashboard/).

## Google Calendar/Meet integration with FluentBooking

**Source:** [https://fluentbooking.com/docs/google-calendar-meet-integration-with-fluent-booking/](https://fluentbooking.com/docs/google-calendar-meet-integration-with-fluent-booking/)

FluentBooking offers seamless integration with your Google Calendar and Google Meet, making your scheduling experience even more efficient and effective. There are two ways to integrate Google Calendar with FluentBooking. We’ll show you both.

## Default Verified App

Unlike any other WordPress appointment plugin, we have a default verified app to integrate Google Calendar.

To use this, go to **Settings** and switch to **Google Calendar/Meet**. Then, choose **Default Verified App** and set your caching time. The recommended setting is 5 minutes however, you can set any duration.

![fluentbooking google calendar integration](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Screenshot-2023-11-24-at-7.55.52 PM.png)

In the **Calendars**section, select the host you want to sync booking events for on Google Calendar/Meet. If you don’t have a host, check out our [documentation](https://fluentbooking.com/docs/how-to-create-a-new-calendar-host/) for guidance on creating one. Now, go to the **Host Settings > Remote Calendar** of that host and click **Connect with Google Calendar/Meet**.

To sync your host’s FluentBooking events with their Google Calendar/Meet, add your host’s Google Account.

![fluentbooking google calendar integration](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Screenshot-2023-11-24-at-8.00.50 PM.png)

Then, give FluentBooking the necessary permissions and click **Continue**.

![fluentbooking google calendar permissions](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Screenshot-2023-11-24-at-8.02.49 PM.png)

That’s it, that’s all you need to integrate Google Calendar with FluentBooking! Now you can choose your calendar and check conflicts to prevent double bookings.

![conflict checking](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Screenshot-2023-11-24-at-8.08.05 PM-1.png)

## Own App (Not Recommended)

Alternatively, you can use our own app to integrate Google Calendar. Here are the steps below:

## Create or Select Project

First, go to the [Google Cloud Console](https://console.cloud.google.com/) and here you have to select a project. You can create a new project too.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Welcome-–-Google-Cloud-console-1.png)

For creating a new project click on the **New Project** button here.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Welcome-–-Google-Cloud-console-2.png)

Give a name to your project and press the **Create** button.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/New-Project-–-Google-Cloud-console-3.png)

You will see a project create confirmation on your notification panel. Now select your project here.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Welcome-–-Google-Cloud-console-4.png)

## Configuring 0Auth Consent

Go to the **APIs & Services**and select the **OAuth consent screen**

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Dashboard-–-Fluent-Booking-Test-–-Google-Cloud-console-5.png)

Here you have to select the **Internal** user type and click on the **Create** button.

> Note: If you’re not using Google Workspace, select External User. Please be aware that the External user type app may break integration with FluentBooking in one to two weeks.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/APIs-Services-–-Fluent-Booking-–-Google-Cloud-console.png)

Here you will see four steps to complete the OAuth consent screen. In the first step, you have to give the App name, User Support Email, Authorized domain, and Developer Contact information then click on the Save & Continue button.

You can simply **Save and Continue** the other three steps.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Edit-app-registration-–-APIs-Services-–-Fluent-Booking-Test-–-Google-Cloud-console-7.png)

After completing the above steps you have to publish the app. Just click on the **Publish App** button.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/APIs-Services-–-F-Booking-–-Google-Cloud-console.png)

## Enabling Google Calendar API

Now go to the**Library** and enable the **Google Calendar API**.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/APIs-Services-–-APIs-Services-–-F-Booking-–-Google-Cloud-console.png)

Here search for the Google Calendar API and simply enable it.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/DRIFT-VIDEO-3815274-3919835-1697199093.gif)

## Create Credentials

Now go to your project and select **Create Credentials > OAuth client ID**

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Credentials-–-APIs-Services-–-Fluent-Booking-Test-–-Google-Cloud-console-12.png)

Here you need to create a web application to get your credentials.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Create-OAuth-client-ID-–-APIs-Services-–-Fluent-Booking-Test-–-Google-Cloud-console-13.png)

Now you need the Authorized redirect URL of FluentBooking. Go to **Settings > Google Calendar** of your FluentBooking plugin and you will get the redirect URL.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/Configure-Google-FluentBooking-1-1.png)

**Copy the URL** and paste it into the **Authorized redirect URL** field.

After that click on the **Create** button and a pop-up will show up with the necessary credentials.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Create-OAuth-client-ID-–-APIs-Services-–-Fluent-Booking-Test-–-Google-Cloud-console-14.png)

Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret** from here.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Credentials-–-APIs-Services-–-Fluent-Booking-Test-–-Google-Cloud-console-21.png)

## Configuring Admin Google Calendar

You must have to add an Admin Google Calender/Meet account in FluentBooking in order to use Google Calender/Meet integration.

To add an Admin Google Calender Account go to your FluentBooking plugin **Settings > Google Calendar** and paste the **Credentials** (*Client ID, Client Secret*) to integrate FluentBooking with Google Calendar/Meet. Click on the **Save** **Google API Configuration** button and you will see a **Success** notification.

> To synchronize your booking events with Google Calendar/Meet you have to connect your Google account to host profiles.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/Configure-Google-FluentBooking-2.png)

## Configuring Host Google Calendar

After setting up an **Admin Google Calendar** account you will be able to add a Google Calendar/Meet account for every individual host also the hosts can add their own Google Account to synchronize their booking events with their Google Calendar/Meet. *You don’t need to re-configure  Google OAuth Credentials to add a Google account for each host, as you have already connected the authenticator app*. Hosts can add multiple Google accounts to manage their booking events.

To synchronize your booking events with Google Calendar/Meet you need to connect a Google account of yours. Go to **Calenders > Host Settings > Remote Calendars** now select the **Connect** **with Google Calendar/Meet** button.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/Booking-Types-FluentBooking-1.png)

You have to log in or select the account you want to synchronize your FluentBooking events.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Sign-in-Google-Accounts.png)

You will see a warning saying, “Google hasn’t verified this app”. Just click on “Advanced” just like in the screenshot below:

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Google-Warning-One.png)

Now click on “Go to {yourWebsite.com}” and it will take you to the next step.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Google-Warning-Two.png)

Lastly, select **all checkboxes** then click on Continue and your integration will be completely done.

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Sign-in-Google-Accounts-2-2.png)

Congrats! You have successfully completed your integration with Google Calendar/Meet. Now Select the**Remote Calendar** and Enable it just like the below:

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Booking-Types-FluentBooking-1.png)

Your bookings can be found on your Google Calendar, just like in the below screenshot:

![](https://fluentbooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/fb-calendar.png)
