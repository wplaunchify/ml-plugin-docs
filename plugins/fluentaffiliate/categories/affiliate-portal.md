# Affiliate Dashboard Overview ​

Welcome to your personal affiliate dashboard! This is your central hub for tracking your performance, generating promotional links, and viewing your earnings. Think of it as your command center for all your affiliate activities.

When you log in to the **Affiliate Portal**, you'll land on this main dashboard page. At the top, you have your main navigation tabs: **Dashboard**, **Links**, **Referrals**, **Payouts**, **Visits**, and **Settings**, which take you to the different parts of your portal.

This guide will walk you through all the features and sections available in your dashboard.

## Accessing the Dashboard ​

To access your Affiliate Dashboard:

1. Log in to your WordPress site
2. Navigate to the Affiliate Portal
3. You'll be taken to your dashboard automatically

### Quick Overview ​

This section at the top of your dashboard displays four key metrics to give you an instant snapshot of your performance:

- **Total Paid:** The total amount of commissions you have already been paid.
- **Total Unpaid:** Approved commissions that you have earned but have not yet been paid out.
- **Total Referrals:** The total number of successful sales you have generated.
- **Conversion Rate:** The percentage of clicks on your links that have turned into successful sales.

![Affiliate Dashboard Overview](/images/affiliate-portal/dashboard/affiliate-dashboard-overview.webp)

### Your Affiliate Link ​

This section is front and center, making it easy to grab your primary affiliate link at any time.

- Simply click the **Copy** button to copy your unique referral URL to your clipboard.
- Share this link with your audience to start earning commissions.
- Click the **Generate Link** button if you want to create a link for a specific page on the site.

### 2. Recent Referrals ​

At the bottom of the page, you’ll see a list of your most recent successful referrals. This table shows the commission Amount, the payment Status (e.g., Paid), the Date of the sale, and a Description of the item purchased. You can click **View All** to go to the main "**Referrals**" tab for a complete history.

### 3. Tips for Using Your Dashboard ​

- **Check Daily:** Monitor your performance metrics daily to see what’s working and track your progress.
- **Generate Custom Links:** Use the "Links" tab to create links for specific products or blog posts you want to promote.
- **Track Payments:** Keep an eye on your "Payouts" tab to see your payment history.
- **Update Your Info:** Make sure your payment email in the "Settings" tab is always up to date to ensure you get paid on time.

## Generating Affiliate Links ​

**Source:** [https://docs.fluentaffiliate.com/guide/affiliate-portal/generating-affiliate-links](https://docs.fluentaffiliate.com/guide/affiliate-portal/generating-affiliate-links)

# Generating Affiliate Links ​

Want to earn from promoting products on the site? As an affiliate, you can easily create your own unique referral links in just a few simple steps. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to generate and manage affiliate links right from your dashboard.

To get started, log in to your affiliate portal and click on the **Links** tab in the main navigation.

## Step 1: Generate Affiliate Link ​

This is the main section for creating a referral link to a specific page on the website, like a particular product, service, page, or blog post.

1. Find the URL of the page you want to promote and **copy** it.
2. **Paste** the full URL into the input field under the "**Generate Affiliate Link**" section.
3. Click the **Generate** button.

Your new, unique referral link will instantly appear below in the **Generated Affiliate Link** area. Click the **Copy** button to copy it to your clipboard.

![Generated Referral Link](/images/affiliate-portal/generating-links/generate-affiliate-link.webp)

## Step 2: Your Affiliate Link & QR Code ​

This section at the bottom of the page always displays your main, default affiliate link.

- **Main Referral Link:** This is your default affiliate link. You can **copy** it quickly by clicking the copy icon next to the URL.
- **QR Code Sharing:** You can also use a custom QR code for your referral link. This is perfect for printed materials like business cards, posters, or sharing directly from your mobile device.
- **Download QR Code:** Click the **Download QR Code** button to save the image to your device, then share it with your audience.

And that's it! You've successfully created a referral link and are ready to promote using both **URLs** and **QR codes**.

## How to Register as an Affiliate ​

**Source:** [https://docs.fluentaffiliate.com/guide/affiliate-portal/how-to-register-as-an-affiliate](https://docs.fluentaffiliate.com/guide/affiliate-portal/how-to-register-as-an-affiliate)

# How to Register as an Affiliate ​

Becoming an affiliate on your site using **Fluent Affiliate** is easy. Whether someone is a long time promoter or just starting their journey, the registration process is quick, simple, and designed for ease.

Let's walk through how a user can register and get started as an affiliate.

## Step 1: Access the Affiliate Dashboard ​

When a user visits your site's **Affiliate Page** usually linked in the menu or footer, they'll see a simple, user friendly interface. From here, they can log in to their affiliate dashboard or click the **Register** button to sign up and become an affiliate.

Click the **Register Now** button to start the sign-up process.

NOTE

Only the site admin can decide where the **Affiliate Page** appears on the site. To learn how to set this up, check out this [article](/guide/settings-and-customization/referral-settings).![Affiliate Registration Page](/images/affiliate-portal/registration/registration-page.webp)

## Step 2: Fill Out the Registration Form ​

After clicking **Register Now**, users will land on the Affiliate Registration page. Here, they'll be asked to provide the necessary details to create their affiliate account. It is divided into clear sections for ease of use.

**Account Information**

This section covers the basic details needed to create the account:

- **Full name**
- **Email Address**
- **Username**
- **Account Password**

**About Yourself**

This part of the form helps the site owner understand more about their potential partners:

- **How will you promote us?:** A field to describe marketing methods and strategies.
- **Website URL:** The user's blog or website link, which helps verify their online presence.
- **Payment Email Address:** The affiliate's PayPal email where their future commission payments will be sent.

**Final Agreement and Submission**

To complete the process, the user must:

1. Check the box to confirm they "**I agree to the terms and conditions.**"
2. Click the **Register** button to submit their application for review.

![Registration Form](/images/affiliate-portal/registration/registration-form.webp)

## Step 3: Welcome to the Affiliate Dashboard ​

After the user submits the form and their application is approved by the **site admin**, they can log in. Upon logging in, they will be directed to their personal **Affiliate Dashboard**.

This dashboard is their central hub to:

- Track performance, including visits, referrals, and earnings.
- Generate unique affiliate links for promoting products.
- View their history of payouts.
- Update their account settings.

To learn more about the Affiliate Dashboard, check out this [article](/guide/affiliate-portal/affiliate-dashboard-overview).

![Affiliate Dashboard](/images/affiliate-portal/registration/registration-success.webp)

And that's it! In just a few simple steps, a new user can join the affiliate program and get everything they need to start promoting and earning.

## Payouts ​

**Source:** [https://docs.fluentaffiliate.com/guide/affiliate-portal/payouts](https://docs.fluentaffiliate.com/guide/affiliate-portal/payouts)

# Payouts ​

In FluentAffiliate, users can easily track their payouts right from the Affiliate Dashboard. To check the payout details, user need to login to their Affiliate account and go to the **Payout** tab from the top menu.

## Payouts ​

This section shows the list of all payouts that have been made to your account. You'll find:

- **Date** – When the payout was processed.
- **Amount** – The total amount you received in that payout.
- **Status** – Whether the payout is Paid, Unpaid, or Pending.
- **Descrption** – This provides details about the payouts.

![Payout Affiliate User Portal](/images/affiliate-portal/payouts/payout-history-view.webp)

All of this helps you quickly understand how much you've earned, what's been paid out, and what's still pending.

## Tracking Referrals ​

**Source:** [https://docs.fluentaffiliate.com/guide/affiliate-portal/tracking-referrals](https://docs.fluentaffiliate.com/guide/affiliate-portal/tracking-referrals)

# Tracking Referrals ​

The Referrals page is where you can see a detailed list of every successful sale or conversion you've generated. It’s the perfect place to track exactly how much you've earned from specific promotions. In this guide, we'll walk you through how affiliate users can view and understand their referrals in FluentAffiliate.

To check your referrals, log in to your affiliate portal and click on the **Referrals** tab in the main navigation.

## Referrals ​

Referral provides a complete history of the commissions you have earned. Each row represents a single referral.

Here is a breakdown of the information in each column:

- **Date:** This shows the exact date and time the referral was recorded.
- **Amount:** This displays the commission you earned for that specific sale.
- **Status:** This tells you the current payment status of your commission. It will show as Paid for commissions you have received, and Unpaid for commissions that are approved but have not yet been sent.
- **Description:** This provides details about the product that was purchased.

![Referrals User Portal](/images/affiliate-portal/referrals/referrals-list-view.webp)

So, now you can easily track your successful promotions and see which products are generating the most income for you.

## Update Your Settings ​

**Source:** [https://docs.fluentaffiliate.com/guide/affiliate-portal/update-your-settings](https://docs.fluentaffiliate.com/guide/affiliate-portal/update-your-settings)

# Update Your Settings ​

The Settings page is where you manage your personal account information, including your payment details and notification preferences. Keeping this information up to date is crucial for receiving your commission payments on time.

To access your settings, log in to your affiliate portal and click on the **Settings** tab in the main navigation.

## Affiliate Settings ​

On this page, you will find the following options:

- **Payment Email (PayPal):** This is the most important field for getting paid. Enter your **PayPal** email address here. All your commission payouts will be sent to this email address.
- **Enable Referral Email Notification:** Check this box if you want to receive an email alert every time you generate a successful referral. If you prefer not to receive these notifications, simply uncheck the box.

After making any changes, be sure to click the **Save Settings** button to apply your updates.

![User Portal Settings](/images/affiliate-portal/profile-settings/profile-settings-page.webp)
